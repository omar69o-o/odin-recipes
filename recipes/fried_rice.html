fried rice

 Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan 
and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, 
vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish
    

 Servings             Cooking                  Preparation
        6                   10min                       20min

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs, lightly beaten (optional)

  • 1 tbsp (15 m) toasted sesame oil (optional)

  • 1 red bell pepper, seeded and diced

  • 1 cup (100 g) snow peas, each cut into three pieces

  • 4 green onions, thinly sliced (white and green parts separate)

  • 2 tbsp (30 ml) peanut oil or canola oil

  • 2 garlic cloves, finely chopped

  • 1/2 tsp (2.5 ml) sambal oelek, or to taste

  • 1 1/2 cups (300 g) long grain white rice, cooked (about 1.25 litres / 5 cups cooked rice)

  • 2 cups (340 g) cooked chicken, diced

  • 3 tbsp (45 ml) soy sauce, or to taste

    •

    instructions

    1. in a wok over high heat, stir-fry the eggs in the sesame oil
stirring vigorouslyfor about 30 seconds, until they are cooked. 
Set aside on a plate. (This step is optional).
            
    2. in the same wok, stir-fry the bell pepper, snow peas, and white part of the onions in the peanut oil until tender.
Add the garlic and sambal oelek and cook for 1 minute. Add the rice, chicken, cooked eggs,
and soy sauce. Heat though over high heat while stirring and adjust the seasoning. 
with the green part of the green onions.
